About Our Church

A Global Family United in Faith

Grace Global Church is a vibrant, multicultural community committed to spreading the Gospel worldwide. Founded in 2001, we have grown from a small gathering to a global movement reaching over 50 countries.

Our mission is simple: to love God, love people, and serve the world. We believe in creating an environment where everyone can encounter God's presence, grow in their faith, and discover their purpose.
